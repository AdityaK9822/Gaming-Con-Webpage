About the Event

Gaming‑Con 2026 will take place from March 20–22, 2026, and is India’s premier gaming and esports convention, uniting gamers, developers, publishers, streamers, and leading tech brands from across the country.

The three‑day event is designed to celebrate gaming culture at every level — from competitive esports and indie game showcases to AAA game launches and hands‑on demos. Attendees can participate in tournaments, interact with creators, explore new technologies, and experience the future of gaming firsthand.
